About

A practice moving between art, pedagogy, technical production, and collaborative media.

Tiago Martins Pinto is an art-tech educator, researcher, and maker working across art education, youth work, audiovisual production, exhibition contexts, and web-based tools.

Based in Helsinki, Finland, the work often links technical fluency with social sensitivity: building situations where code, images, sound, and collective process can support learning, reflection, and shared authorship.

The current research context at Aalto University focuses on STEAM education and informal pedagogies as tools for social justice and educational equity in youth centers.
